# White Wine Lemon Sauce

## Ingredients

* Olive oil
* 1 tbsp garlic
* 1/4 cup lemon juice
* 1/4 cup white wine
* 3 tbsp Butter
* Thyme, finely chopped
* Salt and Pepper

## Preparation

1. In a pan, over medium heat, add olive oil and the garlic up to golden brown.
2. Add lemon and wine.
3. Add butter and reduce it.
4. Add thyme, salt, and pepper.
