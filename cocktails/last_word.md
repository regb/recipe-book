# Last Word

## Ingredients

* 3/4 oz (22ml) Gin
* 3/4 oz (22ml) Lime Juice
* 3/4 oz (22ml) Chartreuse
* 3/4 oz (22ml) Maraschino
* cherry for garnish

## How To

1. Pour gin, lime juice, chartreuse, and maraschino into a cocktail shaker.
2. Add ice and shake.
3. Serve into a cocktail glass, no ice, and garnish with cherry.
