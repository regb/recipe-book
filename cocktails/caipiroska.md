# Caipiroska

## Ingrédients

* 2 ounce (60ml) Vodka
* 2 (thick) tea spoon of sugar
* a lime.

## Marche à suivre

1. Slice the lime in half then in 4, put the pieces in the glass, add the sugar
   on top. Muddle.
2. Fill the glass with crushed ice.
3. Pour the vodka, mix, garnish with a lime wedge.
