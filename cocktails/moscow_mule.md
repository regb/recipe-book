# Moscow Mule

## Ingrédients

* 2 ounce (60ml) Vodka
* 1/4 ounce (10ml) of lime juice
* 3 ounces (90ml) Ginger beer
* (optional) 1 tsp simple syrup
* lime wheel

## Marche à suivre

1. Add crushed ice in the mug (this is served with a mug).
2. Pour all ingredients.
3. Stir, garnish with a lime wheel, and serve.
